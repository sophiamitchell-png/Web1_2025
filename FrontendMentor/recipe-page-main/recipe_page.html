Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well combined.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook gently, moving edges toward center as they set.
  4. Add fillings, fold the omelette in half, and finish cooking for about 30–60 seconds.
  5. Slide onto a plate and serve warm.
Nutrition
Approximate: 277 kcal · 20g protein · 22g fat
