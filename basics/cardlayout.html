The Boston Celtics are a professional basketball team from Boston, Massachusetts, that competes in the NBA and holds the record for the most championships with 18 titles. Founded in 1946, they are one of the NBA's most successful franchises, with iconic eras led by players like Bill Russell and Larry Bird, and a recent 2024 championship to add to their total. The team plays its home games at TD Garden, a modern arena located in Boston.

The Golden State Warriors are a professional basketball team based in San Francisco, California, competing in the NBA's Western Conference. Founded in 1946 as the Philadelphia Warriors, they were one of the league's original teams and won the first championship in 1947. The franchise later relocated to the San Francisco Bay Area in 1962 and became the San Francisco Warriors, before rebranding as the Golden State Warriors in 1971 to represent the entire state of California.

The Minnesota Timberwolves are a professional basketball team in the NBA's Western Conference, based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Founded in 1989, they bring basketball to the state and currently feature star player Anthony Edwards. Their history includes a Western Conference Finals appearance in 2003-04 with Kevin Garnett and they recently returned the popular "Black Trees" uniforms for the 2025-26 season.

The Los Angeles Lakers are a professional basketball team in the NBA, famous for their record-tying 17 NBA championships, iconic purple and gold colors, and a rich history that includes legendary players like Magic Johnson and Kobe Bryant. Originally the Minneapolis Lakers, the team relocated to Los Angeles in 1960 and is one of the NBA's most successful and popular franchises.

The Miami Heat are a professional basketball team in the NBA's Eastern Conference, founded in 1988. They compete in the Southeast Division and have won three NBA championships. The franchise's history includes iconic eras such as the Dwyane Wade era, the LeBron James "Big Three" era, and the Jimmy Butler era, leading the team to multiple NBA Finals appearances, including in 2006, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2020, and 2023. The team is also known for setting the NBA's third-longest winning streak of 27 games during the 2012 and 2013 season.
