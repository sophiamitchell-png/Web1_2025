Synopsis

In the book Dragons Love Tacos by Adam Rubin, a boy discovers that dragons love tacos and throws a taco party to celebrate, but they accidentally serve spicy salsa with the tacos, causing the dragons to breathe fire and burn the house down. The dragons, however, cheerfully help the boy rebuild his house, motivated by their love of tacos and the promise of more delicious food.

About The Author

The book Dragons Love Tacos was written by Adam Rubin and illustrated by Daniel Salmieri. Rubin is a #1 New York Times bestselling author known for his critically acclaimed picture books, while Salmieri is a New York Times bestselling illustrator and also an author.
